We Are

Downstreet

We ensure that there is equity in the housing opportunities available to Vermont households throughout and beyond Central Vermont. We believe that everyone deserves a safe, stable place to call home and that there is more to a home than four walls and a roof. All of our programs and services are developed and executed according to these beliefs.
